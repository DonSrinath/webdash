# **1\. Introduction**

Welcome to WebDash ("we", "our", or "us"). This Privacy Policy explains how we handle information in connection with your use of the WebDash mobile application ("App"). We are committed to protecting your privacy and being transparent about our practices.

By using WebDash, you agree to the terms described in this Privacy Policy. If you do not agree, please discontinue use of the App.

# **2\. About WebDash**

WebDash is a WebView-based application that allows you to browse websites and web-based content directly within the app. The App functions as a browser interface and does not independently collect, store, or transmit your personal data.

# **3\. Information We Collect**

## **3.1 Information We Do NOT Collect**

WebDash is designed with a minimal data footprint. We do not collect:

* Personal identification information (name, email address, phone number)

* Location data

* Camera or microphone data

* Contacts or calendar information

* Financial or payment information

* Device identifiers or advertising IDs

## **3.2 Automatically Handled Data**

WebDash uses the following Android permissions solely to enable core functionality:

* INTERNET — required to load web content within the WebView

* ACCESS\_NETWORK\_STATE — used to check connectivity before loading pages

These permissions do not result in any data being sent to us. They are used exclusively to provide the in-app browsing experience.

## **3.3 Website Data**

When you browse websites within WebDash, the websites you visit may independently collect data about you according to their own privacy policies. WebDash does not control, monitor, or have access to this data. We encourage you to review the privacy policies of any websites you visit through the App.

# **4\. How We Use Information**

Since WebDash does not collect personal data, we do not use your data for any purpose, including:

* Marketing or advertising

* Analytics or profiling

* Selling or sharing with third parties

# **5\. Third-Party Services**

WebDash does not integrate any third-party SDKs, analytics tools, advertising networks, or tracking services. The App has no backend server that receives data from your device.

Any third-party websites or services accessed via the WebView are governed by their own privacy policies and are entirely independent of WebDash.

# **6\. Data Storage and Security**

WebDash does not store your personal data on our servers because we do not collect any. Any browsing session data (such as cookies or cached content) is stored locally on your device by the Android WebView component and is subject to your device's storage and security settings.

You may clear this local data at any time through your device's app settings (Settings \> Apps \> WebDash \> Clear Data / Clear Cache).

# **7\. Children's Privacy**

WebDash does not knowingly collect personal information from children under the age of 13 (or the applicable age of digital consent in your jurisdiction). Since we do not collect any personal data from any users, this applies equally to all age groups.

# **8\. Your Rights**

* Uninstall the App at any time to stop any local data storage
